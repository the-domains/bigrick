Volley in the Valley another resounding success

The St. Helena Hospital Foundation hosted its sixth annual Volley in the Valley Tennis Tournament on May 1-2, benefiting St. Helena Hospital's new Hybrid Operating Room. More than 50 local tennis players participated in the two-day tournament, consisting of four Round Robin doubles tournaments, including men's, women's and mixed, three tennis skill games, dinner under the stars on the Croquet Glen at Meadowood Napa Valley, and an awards ceremony with generously donated player prizes.
