The 40th Anniversary of the Paris Tasting

This year marks four decades since the infamous Paris Tasting of 1976, the one in which French judges were mortified when they picked California wines as victors in blind tastings. The "Judgment of Paris," as the tasting came to be known, put California winemaking on the map, inspiring people all over the globe to take its craft seriously.
