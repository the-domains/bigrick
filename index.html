Massachusetts artist's illustration selected for US Postal Service stamp - The Boston Globe

The US Postal Service has selected a Massachusetts artist's illustration of pinot noir grapes growing on the vine for a new 5-cent stamp. The pen, ink, and watercolor illustration of the deep purple grapes by John Burgoyne, of West Barnstable, was one of about 40,000 submissions the service receives each year, according to Mark Saunders, a postal service spokesman.
