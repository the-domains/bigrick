'Napa mom' touches the world

For nearly a dozen young winemakers from Europe and South America, Napa resident Linda Hansen is like a second mother. During each year's harvest season, Hansen said, hundreds of international winemakers stay with host families in Napa and Sonoma. Hansen has been hosting visiting winemakers since 2009 and typically hosts one to three of these interns at a time.
